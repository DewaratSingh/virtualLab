About Virtual Lab

The Virtual Science Laboratory is designed to make learning interactive and fun. It allows students to perform experiments digitally without expensive equipment, ensuring safe and accessible learning for everyone.

Why Virtual Lab?

Many schools in rural and urban areas lack physical labs or proper resources. Our Virtual Lab bridges this gap by offering offline, low-cost, and easy-to-use simulations that teachers and students can rely on anytime.
